What’s the story behind your name?
Supposedly, my name was only supposed to be “Erica Fernandez.” My two older brothers named me Erica after an anime they loved, Tosho Daimos. So technically, I was named after a robot show. Iconic. Then, on my mother’s side, there’s this unspoken family rule that every granddaughter must have the name “Ruby,” from our grandmother. I mean, sure, what's another name, right? So now I’m Erica Ruby. BUT WAIT, there’s more. As the first granddaughter on my father’s side, they also decided I needed “Ma.”, from my great-grandmother’s name. Because apparently, my name wasn’t long enough yet. And that, friends, is how Erica Fernandez turned into Ma. Erica Ruby Fernandez — a full-length feature film of a name and none of it was my decision.
